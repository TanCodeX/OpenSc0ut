## 🌍 Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in the **OpenSc0ut** community a **harassment-free experience for everyone**, regardless of:

> age · body size · visible or invisible disability · ethnicity · sex characteristics  
> gender identity and expression · level of experience · education · socio-economic status  
> nationality · personal appearance · race · religion · sexual identity and orientation

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an **open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.**

<br />

---

## ✅ Our Standards

### Behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

| 💚 Do | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Empathy** | Show kindness and care toward other community members |
| **Respect** | Honor differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences |
| **Feedback** | Give and gracefully accept constructive criticism |
| **Accountability** | Accept responsibility for mistakes and learn from them |
| **Community first** | Focus on what's best for the overall community |

<br />

### Behavior that is considered unacceptable:

| ❌ Don't | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Sexualization** | Sexualized language, imagery, or unwanted advances of any kind |
| **Harassment** | Trolling, insulting comments, personal or political attacks |
| **Privacy violations** | Publishing others' private information without explicit permission |
| **Intimidation** | Public or private harassment of any kind |
| **Misconduct** | Any conduct inappropriate in a professional setting |

<br />

---

## 👮 Enforcement Responsibilities

Community leaders are responsible for **clarifying and enforcing** our standards of acceptable behavior. They will take **appropriate and fair corrective action** in response to any behavior deemed inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

Community leaders have the right and responsibility to **remove, edit, or reject** comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.

<br />

---

## 🌐 Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within **all community spaces** — including GitHub issues, PRs, discussions, and any official social media — and also when an individual is officially representing the community in public spaces.

## 📜 Enforcement Guidelines

| Severity | Consequence |
|----------|-------------|
| **Correction** | Private warning with clarity on the violation |
| **Warning** | Public warning; restricted interaction for a set period |
| **Temporary Ban** | Temporary ban from all community interaction |
| **Permanent Ban** | Permanent removal from all community spaces |
